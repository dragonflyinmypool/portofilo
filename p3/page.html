Design Challange

Analyze a city block. Look at the space, movement, environment and other factors. Make an improvement to the city block, based on the analyzes.

My Design

I found that the interior of the block was being neglected, undervalued and could be put to better use. I created an internal green space that could be used for walking and movement. This space could also be connected to other similar blocks providing a valuable walking network through the city.
